Result summary


Taking part is optional
You will only appear on the ladder once you've played at least 1 game
You have an initial rating of 1500
Gain lots of points by beating someone higher rated than you. Fewer points if they are weaker
Lose lots of points by losing to someone lower rated than you. Fewer points if they are stronger
When live, a password will be required to submit a result. Any password works at the moment
The men's singles ladder is password protected as some rated games have already taken place
